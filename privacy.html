Privacy Policy

We keep it simple. Here's exactly what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it.

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Teengro ("we", "our", "us") is an AI-powered tutoring platform for students in grades 8–12, available at teengro.in. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how your data is handled.

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of Teengro, including users of our website and mobile app.

Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Name and username you provide during signup
  • Email address used to create your account
  • Password (stored securely — we never see it in plain text)
  • Profile picture, if you choose to upload one

Learning Data

  • Class, board, and subject preferences you set
  • Questions you ask the AI tutor
  • Study time and usage activity
  • Day streak and session history

Technical Data

  • Time spent on the app

How We Use Your Data

  • To create and manage your account
  • To personalise your learning experience (class, board, subject)
  • To power the AI tutor responses
  • To track your study streaks and progress
  • To improve Teengro's features and performance
  • To send important account-related emails (e.g. password reset)

We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising.

Third-Party Services

Teengro uses the following trusted third-party services to operate:

  • Supabase — secure database and authentication
  • Google Analytics — anonymous usage analytics to improve the app
  • Google Fonts — typography rendering
  • AI providers — to generate AI tutor responses

Each of these services has their own privacy policies and data handling practices.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, which uses industry-standard encryption. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. We take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorised access.

Children's Privacy

Teengro is designed for students in grades 8–12 (typically ages 13–18). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 without parental consent. If you believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Your Rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of Teengro after changes means you accept the updated policy.
